Dragon Roll Sushi

Ever wanted to taste the rarest, yummiest Sushi? Well, here you can.

​

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp Tempura
  • Avacado
  • Cucumber
  • Sushi Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Spicy Mayo
  • Unagi Sauce
​
    ​
  1. Clean the Shrimp and pop them into the oven or 20 minutes
  2. Cut the Avacado to small pieces
  3. Slice the Cucumbers to thin pieces and remove the seeds
  4. Steam the Sushi Rice for 30 minutes
  5. Cut half a sheet of seaweed and roll it
  6. Layer the seaweed with the spicy mayo
  7. Get a boil and put the Unagi Sauce in it for dipping
